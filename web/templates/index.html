RRA = Random Reddit Account

The username is generated from animal names, adjectives and city names totalling around 9,990,695 possible different usernames.

Unfortunately the maximum username length is 20 characters so sometimes we can't use words from all 3 of those things (animal, adjective, city), in this case a random number is inserted somewhere into the word.

Please note that the registration happens server-side, this essentially means that sometimes the app gets blocked from reddit for 5-10 minutes!
